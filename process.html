The Process

A proven path to market.
Four stages. One partner.

Each stage is designed to de-risk your entry into Thailand while building the operational foundation you will rely on for years.

STEP 01

Consultation & Discovery

We start with a deep-dive conversation — in Chinese or English, whichever you prefer. You tell us about your brand, your goals for the Thai market, and the challenges you have faced so far. We listen. Then we ask the questions that matter: What is your timeline? Your budget reality? Your risk tolerance? By the end of this stage, we both know whether there is a real fit — and we will tell you honestly if there is not.

Deliverable: Opportunity Assessment & Fit Memo
STEP 02

Strategy & Roadmap

Once we commit to working together, we build a detailed market entry and operations strategy. This includes competitive landscape mapping, regulatory pathway analysis, logistics and fulfillment architecture, and a phased timeline with clear milestones. We do not deliver a 200-page PDF you will never read. You get a concise, actionable roadmap — and we walk through every section with you, line by line, to make sure it aligns with your internal capabilities and constraints.

Deliverable: Strategic Roadmap & Phased Execution Plan
STEP 03

Local Execution

This is where most consultancies stop — and where we begin. We move from the roadmap into the real world: visiting warehouse sites with you on video call, sitting in negotiation rooms with potential local partners, briefing Thai KOLs in their native language, and managing the daily operations that make market entry real. Our bilingual team ensures nothing is lost in translation — literally or culturally. You stay informed through a shared dashboard and weekly syncs, but you do not have to be in Bangkok to make things happen.

Deliverable: Live Operations — Warehouses, Partners & Campaigns
STEP 04

Long-Term Partnership

Market entry is not the finish line — it is the starting block. Once your operations are live, we stay embedded as your local operating team. We monitor partner performance, adjust strategy as market conditions shift, handle unexpected issues before they become crises, and proactively identify new opportunities for growth. Our model is not built on one-off projects. It is built on multi-year relationships where we become an extension of your team — accountable, responsive, and as invested in your success in Thailand as you are.

Deliverable: Ongoing Operations, Quarterly Reviews & Growth Roadmap

Our typical engagement runs 12–24 months, with the first tangible results visible within 4–6 weeks of execution. Every relationship starts with a no-obligation consultation.

Our Commitment

What you can expect from us
at every stage of the process.

Three principles that govern how we work — whether we are on a discovery call, in a negotiation room, or managing your operations day to day.

Trust

We tell you what you need to hear — not what you want to hear. If a market opportunity is not right for your brand, we say so. If a partner fails our vetting, they never reach your inbox. Our reputation depends on the quality of every introduction and every recommendation we make.

Execution

We measure ourselves by what gets done — not by slide decks delivered, not by meetings held. A warehouse is either operational or it is not. A creator campaign is either live or it is not. We hold ourselves to that standard at every stage, and we report progress against concrete milestones, not activity metrics.

Partnership

We are not a vendor you manage; we are a partner you trust. That means we are proactive — flagging risks before they materialize, surfacing opportunities you had not considered, and treating your budget with the same care we would our own. When you succeed in Thailand, we succeed. That alignment is the foundation of every engagement we take on.
