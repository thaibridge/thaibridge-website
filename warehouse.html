Why You Need This

Warehousing in Thailand isn't as simple
as renting shelf space.

Chinese brands face real operational challenges that most Thailand-entry guides never mention.

01

Can't Find Trustworthy Warehouses

From abroad, you have no way to verify a warehouse's actual condition, operational standards, or reliability. Online listings are outdated and reviews can be fabricated.

02

Language Barrier with Thai Operators

Warehouse staff in Thailand speak Thai. Miscommunication about SKU handling, labeling requirements, or delivery windows leads to stockouts, returns, and angry customers.

03

No Local Escalation When Issues Happen

When a shipment is delayed or a batch arrives damaged, sending WeChat messages from Shenzhen at 11pm gets you nowhere. You need someone local who can walk in the door.

04

Unpredictable Costs

Warehouses quote one price but pile on storage surcharges, pick-pack fees, and return handling charges that were never disclosed. Without local knowledge, you cannot benchmark.

05

No Daily Fulfillment Management

Even after signing a contract, someone needs to manage daily order flows, handle exceptions, reconcile inventory, and ensure SLA compliance. That someone cannot be you — at midnight in a different time zone.

How We Work

From requirements to daily operations
in five steps.

A proven process that gets your products on Thai shelves — and keeps them moving.

1

Discovery

We map your product characteristics (dimensions, weight, turnover rate, temperature requirements), order volume projections, and special handling needs. We identify which Thai regions your customers are in and what delivery SLA you need. Output: a detailed Requirements Document that warehouses bid against.

2

Warehouse Matching

We shortlist 3-5 warehouses from our personally-vetted network that match your requirements. We conduct site visits, inspect facilities, review WMS capabilities, and run reference checks with current clients. You receive a side-by-side comparison matrix with our candid recommendation.

3

Negotiation

We negotiate contract terms in Thai — storage rates, pick-pack fees, last-mile rates, minimums, payment terms, SLA commitments, and penalty clauses. We ensure every cost is documented. No surprises. We translate the final contract into Chinese and walk you through every clause.

4

Onboarding

We manage the entire integration: WMS setup, SKU master data upload, packaging specifications, return handling SOPs, and test shipments. We train warehouse staff on your brand requirements. We run a 2-week parallel operations period before full go-live.

5

Ongoing Operations

Daily order reconciliation, weekly inventory reports, monthly SLA reviews. We handle all warehouse communication in Thai. We escalate issues before they impact your customers. Monthly operations report delivered in Chinese — with KPIs, exception logs, and cost analysis.

FAQ

Common questions about our
warehouse service.

Straight answers from our operations team in Bangkok.

How long does it take to get operational?+
Most clients are operational within 4-6 weeks from agreement. The timeline depends on warehouse availability, contract negotiation speed, and integration complexity. We have expedited clients to go-live in 2 weeks when the warehouse is already in our network and the integration scope is straightforward. For complex WMS integrations or multi-warehouse setups, plan for 6-8 weeks.
Do you mark up warehouse fees?+
No. We never take commissions or kickbacks from warehouses. You pay the warehouse directly at the rates we negotiate. Our fee is a separate, transparent monthly retainer for operations management. This alignment means we are incentivized to find you the best warehouse at the best rate — not the one that pays us the highest referral fee. You will always see the warehouse's actual invoice.
What happens if the warehouse underperforms?+
We track SLA performance daily. If a warehouse misses targets — late shipments, damaged goods, inventory discrepancies — we escalate immediately with the warehouse management and drive corrective action. If performance does not improve within an agreed remediation period, we initiate a transition to one of our backup warehouse partners. Because we maintain our own vetted network, we can usually switch warehouses without disrupting your operations. You are never locked in.
Which cities do you cover?+
Our primary coverage is Bangkok and its surrounding logistics zones (Samut Prakan, Chachoengsao, Pathum Thani), which is where most e-commerce and distribution activity is centered. We also cover Chiang Mai (Northern Thailand), Chonburi / Laem Chabang (Eastern Seaboard / port zone), and Phuket (Southern Thailand). For nationwide last-mile, we partner with Thailand's major courier networks. If your business requires warehousing in a specific province, tell us — we will assess feasibility and add it to our network if viable.
Can you handle returns and reverse logistics?+
Yes. Returns management is a standard part of our service. We define return handling SOPs during onboarding — including inspection criteria, restocking rules, disposal procedures, and refund triggers. Our team manages the entire reverse logistics flow: receiving returns, inspecting condition, updating inventory, processing refunds per your policy, and reporting return reasons so you can identify product or listing issues. This is especially critical for e-commerce brands, where return rates can reach 15-30% in some categories.
