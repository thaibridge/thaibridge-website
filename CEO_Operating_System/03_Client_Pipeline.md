## Pipeline Stages

```
Lead → Contacted → Meeting → Proposal → Negotiation → Won → Long-term Partner
  ↓         ↓          ↓         ↓           ↓
 Lost      Lost       Lost      Lost        Lost
```

| Stage | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| **Lead** | Identified potential client. No contact yet. |
| **Contacted** | Initial outreach sent. Waiting for response. |
| **Meeting** | First meeting/call completed. Qualified opportunity. |
| **Proposal** | Tailored proposal or scope of work delivered. |
| **Negotiation** | Terms, pricing, or scope being discussed. |
| **Won** | Signed agreement. Onboarding begun. |
| **Long-term Partner** | Active client ≥ 6 months with ongoing retainer. |
| **Lost** | Deal closed without signing. Reason documented. |
