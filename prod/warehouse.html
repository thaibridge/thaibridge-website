Why You Need This · 为什么需要这个服务

Warehousing in Thailand isn't as simple
as renting shelf space.
在泰国做仓储不是租个货架那么简单。

Chinese brands face real operational challenges that most Thailand-entry guides never mention.
中国品牌面临的真实运营难题，大多数泰国市场进入指南都未曾提及。

01

Can't Find Trustworthy Warehouses
找不到可靠的仓库

From abroad, you have no way to verify a warehouse's actual condition, operational standards, or reliability. Online listings are outdated and reviews can be fabricated. · 身在海外，你无法核实仓库的真实状况、运营标准和可靠性。网上信息早已过时，评价也可能是编造的。

02

Language Barrier with Thai Operators
与泰国运营方的语言障碍

Warehouse staff in Thailand speak Thai. Miscommunication about SKU handling, labeling requirements, or delivery windows leads to stockouts, returns, and angry customers. · 泰国仓库员工只说泰语。关于SKU处理、标签要求或配送时限的沟通失误会导致缺货、退货和客户投诉。

03

No Local Escalation When Issues Happen
出问题时没有本地人员能现场处理

When a shipment is delayed or a batch arrives damaged, sending WeChat messages from Shenzhen at 11pm gets you nowhere. You need someone local who can walk in the door. · 当货件延误或批次到货时发现损坏，晚上11点从深圳发微信消息解决不了任何问题。你需要一个能亲自上门处理的本地人。

04

Unpredictable Costs
成本不可预测

Warehouses quote one price but pile on storage surcharges, pick-pack fees, and return handling charges that were never disclosed. Without local knowledge, you cannot benchmark. · 仓库报价看起来很便宜，但会附加各种从未告知的仓储附加费、拣货包装费和退货处理费。没有本地经验，你根本无法判断价格是否合理。

05

No Daily Fulfillment Management
缺乏日常履约管理

Even after signing a contract, someone needs to manage daily order flows, handle exceptions, reconcile inventory, and ensure SLA compliance. That someone cannot be you — at midnight in a different time zone. · 即使签了合同，也需要有人管理每日订单流转、处理异常情况、核对库存、确保SLA达标。这个人不能是你——在另一个时区的深夜里。

Negotiation · 合同谈判

Q: How long does it take to get operational?
问：从签约到运营需要多长时间？+
Most clients are operational within 4-6 weeks from agreement. The timeline depends on warehouse availability, contract negotiation speed, and integration complexity. We have expedited clients to go-live in 2 weeks when the warehouse is already in our network and the integration scope is straightforward. For complex WMS integrations or multi-warehouse setups, plan for 6-8 weeks.

大多数客户从签约到运营需要4-6周。时间取决于仓库可用性、合同谈判速度和对接复杂度。如果仓库已在我们网络中且对接范围简单，我们有客户最快2周上线。对于复杂的WMS对接或多仓库部署，请预留6-8周。
Q: Do you mark up warehouse fees?
问：仓储费你们加价吗？+
No. We never take commissions or kickbacks from warehouses. You pay the warehouse directly at the rates we negotiate. Our fee is a separate, transparent monthly retainer for operations management. This alignment means we are incentivized to find you the best warehouse at the best rate — not the one that pays us the highest referral fee. You will always see the warehouse's actual invoice.

不加价。我们绝不从仓库方拿佣金或回扣。您按照我们谈好的费率直接付给仓库。我们的费用是独立、透明的月度运营管理服务费。这种模式确保我们的利益与您一致——为您找最合适的仓库、最优的费率，而不是给我们返佣最高的仓库。您始终能看到仓库的真实账单。
Q: What happens if the warehouse underperforms?
问：如果仓库表现不达标怎么办？+
We track SLA performance daily. If a warehouse misses targets — late shipments, damaged goods, inventory discrepancies — we escalate immediately with the warehouse management and drive corrective action. If performance does not improve within an agreed remediation period, we initiate a transition to one of our backup warehouse partners. Because we maintain our own vetted network, we can usually switch warehouses without disrupting your operations. You are never locked in.

我们每天追踪SLA表现。如果仓库未达标——发货延迟、货物损坏、库存差异——我们会立即与仓库管理层升级沟通并推动纠改。如果在约定的整改期内表现没有改善，我们将启动切换到备选仓库合作伙伴的流程。因为我们维护着自己的筛选网络，通常可以在不中断您运营的情况下完成仓库切换。您永远不会被锁定在一个仓库上。
Q: Which cities do you cover?
问：你们覆盖哪些城市？+
Our primary coverage is Bangkok and its surrounding logistics zones (Samut Prakan, Chachoengsao, Pathum Thani), which is where most e-commerce and distribution activity is centered. We also cover Chiang Mai (Northern Thailand), Chonburi / Laem Chabang (Eastern Seaboard / port zone), and Phuket (Southern Thailand). For nationwide last-mile, we partner with Thailand's major courier networks. If your business requires warehousing in a specific province, tell us — we will assess feasibility and add it to our network if viable.

我们的主要覆盖区域是曼谷及其周边物流区（北榄府、北柳府、巴吞他尼府），这是大多数电商和分销活动的中心。我们还覆盖清迈（泰北）、春武里/林查班（东部沿海/港口区）和普吉（泰南）。对于全国范围的末端配送，我们与泰国主流快递网络合作。如果您的业务需要在特定省份设仓，请告知我们——我们会评估可行性，并在可行的情况下纳入我们的网络。
Q: Can you handle returns and reverse logistics?
问：你们能处理退货和逆向物流吗？+
Yes. Returns management is a standard part of our service. We define return handling SOPs during onboarding — including inspection criteria, restocking rules, disposal procedures, and refund triggers. Our team manages the entire reverse logistics flow: receiving returns, inspecting condition, updating inventory, processing refunds per your policy, and reporting return reasons so you can identify product or listing issues. This is especially critical for e-commerce brands, where return rates can reach 15-30% in some categories.

可以。退货管理是我们服务的标配。我们在上线对接阶段就制定退货处理SOP——包括验货标准、重新入库规则、报废流程和退款触发条件。我们团队管理整个逆向物流流程：接收退货、检查状态、更新库存、按您的政策处理退款，并报告退货原因帮助您发现产品或listing问题。这对电商品牌尤其关键，某些品类的退货率可达15%-30%。
