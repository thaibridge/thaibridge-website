Why You Need This · 为什么需要这项服务

Finding the right Thai business partner
from abroad is a minefield.
从海外寻找合适的泰国商业合作伙伴犹如走进雷区。

The wrong partner can destroy your Thailand entry. Here are the real risks Chinese brands face. · 选错合作伙伴足以毁掉您的泰国市场进入计划。以下是中国品牌面临的实际风险。

01

No Way to Verify Partners from Abroad · 海外无法验证合作伙伴真伪

LinkedIn profiles, trade show meetings, and WeChat introductions tell you nothing about a Thai company's actual reputation, financial health, or operational capability. You are making a multi-year commitment based on a 60-minute meeting and a polished deck. · 领英资料、展会面谈、微信介绍都无法告诉你一家泰国公司的真实声誉、财务状况或运营能力。你在用一次60分钟的会面和一份精美的PPT，做一个涉及多年的承诺。

02

Cross-Cultural Negotiation Breaks Deals · 跨文化谈判导致合作破裂

Thai business negotiation follows different norms — indirect communication, relationship-first pacing, and face-saving conventions. Chinese brands that push for fast, direct terms often offend partners or misunderstand what was actually agreed. Deals fall apart before they start. · 泰国商务谈判遵循不同的规则——间接沟通、关系优先、注重面子。中国品牌若急于推进直接条款，往往会冒犯合作伙伴或误解真正达成的共识。合作还没开始就破裂了。

03

No One to Escalate to When Problems Arise · 出问题时没有可升级投诉的对象

When your distributor misses targets, your supplier ships defective product, or your JV partner is not contributing as agreed — who do you call? Without a local escalation partner, disputes fester. Resentment builds. The partnership dies slowly and expensively. · 当经销商未完成目标、供应商发来有问题的产品、或合资伙伴未按约定出资时——你该找谁？没有本地的升级投诉渠道，纠纷就会恶化，怨恨不断积累，合作关系缓慢而昂贵地走向死亡。

04

The Cost of a Bad Partnership Is Enormous · 选错合作伙伴代价巨大

A failed distribution deal wastes 12-18 months of market opportunity. A bad supplier relationship can destroy product quality and brand reputation. A broken JV can involve legal costs, asset disputes, and regulatory complications. Recovery is measured in years and millions of baht. · 一个失败的经销合作会浪费12-18个月的市场窗口。一个糟糕的供应商关系会毁掉产品质量和品牌声誉。一个破裂的合资企业可能涉及法律诉讼、资产纠纷和监管问题。恢复期以年和百万泰铢计。

Three paths to a Thai business partner.

How DIY networking, traditional matchmaking agencies, and ThaiBridge compare across the dimensions that determine partnership success. · 自建网络、传统配对中介与ThaiBridge在决定合作成功的关键维度上如何对比。

Partner Verification & Due Diligence · 合作伙伴验证与尽职调查

DIY
Attend trade shows, search online directories, ask your network for introductions. You cannot verify claims about revenue, capabilities, or references. Due diligence is limited to what the partner voluntarily shares — and what Google Translate can decode from Thai-language documents.
Agency
Maintain a database of "partners" who pay to be listed. Due diligence is limited to checking that the company exists and has a website. References are supplied by the partner. The agency's incentive is closing a match, not ensuring a good fit.
ThaiBridge
Mandatory site visits to every partner. We verify business registration, financial standing, facility condition, reference clients (contacted independently), and reputation in the industry. We produce a detailed Partner Evaluation Report — including risks, red flags, and our candid recommendation. If a partner does not meet our standard, they never reach your desk.

Negotiation & Deal Structure · 谈判与交易结构

DIY
Negotiate in English across cultural gaps. You may concede on terms that are standard in Thailand without knowing it. Or you may demand terms that are culturally offensive and walk away from a good partner over a misunderstanding.
Agency
Arrange the introduction, sit in on one meeting, then step back. The actual negotiation is between you and a partner you barely know. The agency collects their fee regardless of whether the deal terms are fair or sustainable.
ThaiBridge
We facilitate every negotiation — bilingually, in person. We know what terms are market-standard in Thailand and what is negotiable. We ensure both sides understand exactly what they are committing to. We flag terms that are unusual, risky, or unfavorable — before you sign. When cultural friction arises, we de-escalate in real time.

Post-Deal Stewardship · 签约后持续管理

DIY
Once the contract is signed, you are on your own. If the partner underperforms, you have no leverage except legal action — which is slow, expensive, and conducted in a foreign legal system you do not understand.
Agency
The agency's job ends at contract signing. Post-deal issues are not their problem. If the partnership fails 3 months in, the agency will happily find you a new partner — for another fee. They are incentivized by churn, not longevity.
ThaiBridge
We stay actively involved for 6-12 months post-deal. We attend quarterly business reviews, track KPIs against the partnership agreement, mediate disputes, and manage course corrections. If the partnership is working, we gradually step back. If it is not, we diagnose the issue and drive resolution — or begin transition planning. Our success metric is partnership longevity, not match volume.

Incentive Alignment · 利益一致性

DIY
Your incentive is clear: find a great partner. The challenge is that you lack the local knowledge, language, and network to evaluate whether a partner is truly great — or just great at presenting to foreign companies.
Agency
Agencies often take commissions from both sides — a fee from you and a "success fee" from the Thai partner. This means they are incentivized to match you with the partner who pays the highest commission, not the partner who best fits your business.
ThaiBridge
Zero partner-side commissions. Ever. Our fee is paid entirely by you, on a fixed retainer basis. We have no financial incentive to favor one partner over another. Our only incentive is finding you a partner who delivers long-term results — because that is what earns us your continued business.
How We Work · 我们的工作方式

From your requirements to a live
partnership in five steps.
从您的需求到实际合作落地，五步到位。

A rigorous process designed to eliminate the risk of a bad match before you commit. · 一套严谨的流程，旨在签约前彻底消除选错合作伙伴的风险。

1

Requirements Definition · 需求定义

We document exactly what you need from a Thai partner: category expertise, geographic coverage, channel relationships (modern trade, traditional trade, e-commerce), financial expectations, exclusivity requirements, and timeline. We pressure-test your assumptions — are you looking for the right type of partner for your stage? Output: an Ideal Partner Profile that defines what "good" looks like.

2

Partner Sourcing · 合作伙伴筛选

We source 10-20 potential partners through our local network, industry associations, trade data, and direct outreach — all conducted in Thai. We screen each candidate against your Ideal Partner Profile and produce a Longlist Summary with our initial assessment of fit, capability, and potential red flags. You select 5-8 partners for detailed due diligence.

3

Due Diligence · 尽职调查

For each shortlisted partner, we conduct: site visit to their office/facility, business registration verification, financial health assessment (where available), reference calls with 3-5 of their existing business partners (not references they supply — we find our own), and reputation check within their industry. Output: a Partner Evaluation Report ranking each candidate with specific strengths, weaknesses, and risks.

4

Introduction & Negotiation · 引荐与谈判

We arrange in-person meetings in Bangkok (or via video if preferred). We attend every meeting — facilitating in both Thai and Chinese/English, bridging cultural norms, and ensuring both sides communicate clearly. We manage the negotiation process: term sheet, LOI, due diligence from the partner's side, and final contract. We translate all key documents and flag any terms that deviate from market standards.

5

Partnership Launch & Stewardship · 合作启动与持续管理

Post-signing, we stay embedded for 6-12 months: facilitating the first 90 days of operations, attending QBRs, tracking KPIs against the partnership agreement, mediating any disputes, and managing course corrections. We deliver a 90-Day Health Check and ongoing monthly stewardship reports. If the partnership is thriving, we transition to a lighter-touch monitoring model. If it is struggling, we diagnose and fix — or manage an orderly transition.

How long does it take to find a partner? · 找到合作伙伴需要多长时间？+
The full process typically takes 8-16 weeks from requirements definition to a signed agreement, depending on partner availability and negotiation complexity. Sourcing and due diligence takes 4-6 weeks. Introduction, negotiation, and contracting takes another 4-8 weeks. Highly specialized partner requirements (e.g., a pharmaceutical distributor with cold-chain capability in Northern Thailand) may take longer. We will give you a realistic timeline during the scoping phase — and we will not rush a match just to close faster. · 完整流程通常需要8-16周，从需求定义到签署协议，取决于合作伙伴的可获取性和谈判复杂度。筛选和尽职调查需要4-6周。引荐、谈判和签约需要另外4-8周。如果合作伙伴要求非常特殊，可能需要更长时间。我们会在范围确认阶段提供实际时间表——不会为了加快结案而仓促配对。
What if the partnership doesn't work out? · 如果合作不成功怎么办？+
This is exactly why our 6-12 month stewardship period exists. If the partnership is underperforming, we diagnose the root cause — is it a capability issue, a commitment issue, or a mismatch in expectations? We mediate a corrective action plan with clear milestones. If the partner cannot or will not improve, we manage the transition — including identifying replacement partners and ensuring a clean handover. Our stewardship fee covers this work. We do not charge a new sourcing fee for a replacement within the first 12 months if the failure is due to partner underperformance verified by our KPI tracking. · 这正是我们提供6-12个月持续管理期的原因。如果合作表现不佳，我们诊断根本原因——是能力问题、投入问题还是预期不匹配？我们协调制定有明确里程碑的纠正行动计划。如果合作伙伴不能或不愿改善，我们管理过渡流程——包括寻找替代合作伙伴并确保平稳交接。我们的管理费已覆盖此项工作。在首12个月内若因合作伙伴表现不佳（经我们的KPI跟踪确认）导致失败，我们不收取新的筛选费用。
Do you work on commission from either side? · 你们从任何一方收取佣金吗？+
No. We charge a fixed retainer to you and accept zero commissions, referral fees, or success fees from any partner we introduce. This is fundamental to our model. We disclose this policy to every partner we engage — so they know our loyalty is to you. If a partner offers us a commission, we decline and disclose the offer to you. This alignment means you can trust that every recommendation is based on fit, not financial incentive. · 不。我们只向您收取固定服务费，不接受来自任何我们引荐的合作伙伴的佣金、推荐费或成功费。这是我们模式的基础。我们向每一个合作的伙伴公开此政策——让他们知道我们的忠诚只属于您。如果有合作伙伴向我们提供佣金，我们拒绝并告知您。这种利益一致性意味着您可以信任每一个推荐都是基于匹配度，而非金钱激励。
Can you help with JV structuring and legal setup? · 你们能协助合资企业架构和法务设立吗？+
We facilitate the commercial aspects of JV formation — partner identification, due diligence, negotiation of commercial terms, and post-formation stewardship. For legal structuring (shareholder agreements, FBA compliance, BOI applications), we work alongside your legal counsel or can recommend trusted Thai corporate law firms from our network. We do not provide legal advice directly, but we ensure the commercial deal terms are correctly reflected in legal documentation and that both sides understand their obligations. Think of us as your commercial representative — not your attorney. · 我们促进合资企业成立的商业层面——合作伙伴识别、尽职调查、商业条款谈判和合作成立后的管理。对于法务架构（股东协议、FBA合规、BOI申请），我们与您的法律顾问协同工作，或可从我们网络中推荐可信赖的泰国公司法务事务所。我们不直接提供法律建议，但确保商业条款正确反映在法律文件中，且双方理解各自的义务。把我看作您的商业代表——而非您的律师。
What industries do you cover? · 你们覆盖哪些行业？+
Our core coverage includes consumer goods (FMCG, beauty, personal care, supplements, food and beverage), consumer electronics, home and lifestyle products, automotive parts and accessories, and industrial supplies. We have particular depth in categories where Chinese brands have a strong product advantage. If your industry is outside these areas, tell us — we will assess whether our network covers your category and give you a candid answer. We will not take on an engagement where we cannot add value through our existing network and expertise. · 我们的核心覆盖范围包括消费品（快消品、美妆、个人护理、保健品、食品饮料）、消费电子、家居生活用品、汽车零部件和工业用品。我们尤其深耕中国品牌具有产品优势的品类。如果您的行业不在这些范围内，请告诉我们——我们会评估我们的网络是否覆盖您的品类并给出坦诚的答复。我们不会承接无法通过现有网络和专业知识为您增加价值的项目。
