Why You Need This · 为什么需要这项服务

Setting up operations in Thailand
is a full-time job. Actually, it's four.
在泰国建立运营是一个全职工作。实际上，是四个。

You cannot run Thai operations from a desk in Shenzhen. Here is what breaks when you try. · 你无法在深圳的办公桌前运营泰国业务。以下是你尝试后会出现的各种问题。

01

Setting Up Operations in a Foreign Country Is a Full-Time Job · 在异国建立运营是一份全职工作

Finding a warehouse, hiring creators, meeting distributors, understanding regulations — these are not side projects. Each workstream requires local presence, Thai language, and domain expertise. Assigning one person on your team to "figure out Thailand" is a recipe for burnout and missed deadlines. · 找仓库、招创作者、见经销商、理解法规——这些不是副业。每个业务线都需要本地存在、泰语能力和领域专业知识。派一个人来'搞定泰国'是导致职业倦怠和错过截止日期的配方。

02

Dozens of Interdependent Workstreams · 数十条相互依赖的业务线

Warehouse setup affects your e-commerce launch timeline. Creator campaigns need product in market. Distributor negotiations depend on market research findings. Every workstream is connected — and managing the interdependencies from abroad, across a language barrier, is nearly impossible. · 仓库设立影响电商上线时间。创作者营销需要产品已上市。经销商谈判依赖市场研究结果。每条业务线都是相互连接的——跨越语言障碍从海外管理这些相互依赖关系，几乎是不可能的。

03

Every Workstream Requires Thai Language · 每条业务线都需要泰语

Warehouse contracts are in Thai. Creator agreements are in Thai. Distributor negotiations happen in Thai. Regulatory filings are in Thai. You cannot manage four parallel workstreams through Google Translate and a part-time interpreter. You need a bilingual team embedded in the country. · 仓库合同是泰语的。创作者协议是泰语的。经销商谈判用泰语进行。监管文件是泰语的。你无法通过谷歌翻译和一个兼职翻译来管理四条并行业务线。你需要一个嵌入当地的双语团队。

04

No Single Point of Accountability · 没有单一问责点

You hire a warehouse consultant, a creator agency, a business matchmaker, and a research firm. Four vendors. Four contracts. Four points of contact. Four different incentives. When things go wrong, each vendor blames the others. You are left coordinating between four parties who have no incentive to cooperate — and every incentive to protect their own scope. · 你请了一个仓库顾问、一个创作者代理、一个商务配对中介和一个研究公司。四个供应商，四份合同，四个对接人，四种不同的利益诉求。当出问题时，每个供应商都指责其他方。你被留在中间，协调着四个没有合作动机的各方——各方都只想保护自己的业务范围。

Team Structure & Coordination · 团队结构与协调

DIY / Internal Team
You assign one or two people to "manage Thailand." They handle everything — or try to. They are not in Thailand. They do not speak Thai. They are also doing their day job. Workstreams stall for weeks because one person cannot be in four places at once. Burnout is inevitable. Turnover resets everything.
Multiple Agencies
You hire a warehouse consultant, a creator agency, a matchmaker, and a researcher — four separate vendors. You become the project manager, coordinating between four parties who do not communicate with each other. When the creator agency needs product samples for a shoot and the warehouse has not received them yet, you are the one making late-night WeChat calls to both.
ThaiBridge
One team handles everything. One point of contact for you. Internally, our warehouse, creator, matching, and research teams coordinate daily — in the same office, in the same language. When one workstream affects another, we catch it before it becomes your problem. You get a weekly summary of all workstreams. You attend one review call, not four.

Communication & Reporting · 沟通与汇报

DIY / Internal Team
Your team communicates with Thai counterparts in English — with varying levels of success. Misunderstandings compound. Status updates are inconsistent. You have no standardized reporting, so you cannot compare progress across workstreams or months.
Multiple Agencies
Each agency has their own reporting format, cadence, and definition of "done." One sends a PDF monthly. Another sends WhatsApp messages sporadically. A third goes silent for three weeks and then delivers a 40-page deck. You spend hours each week just consolidating status updates.
ThaiBridge
Weekly Progress Reports — standardized across all workstreams. Monthly Performance Dashboard with KPIs, milestones, burn rate, and risks. Quarterly Strategy Review where we step back and assess overall progress against your Thailand entry roadmap. All reporting is bilingual — in Chinese with English summaries. One format. One cadence. One source of truth.

Cost Structure & Flexibility · 成本结构与灵活性

DIY / Internal Team
Hiring even one full-time person in Bangkok costs $2,000-$4,000 per month (salary, benefits, office, visa). Plus you need to manage them. Plus they need to be bilingual and experienced in your category — a rare and expensive combination. And if they leave, you start over.
Multiple Agencies
Each agency has minimum commitments, different billing cycles, and scope limitations. The warehouse consultant charges a project fee but does not do creators. The creator agency charges a campaign retainer but does not do research. Total cost is unpredictable and scope gaps are inevitable.
ThaiBridge
Monthly retainer. Clear scope. Scale up or down each quarter. You get a full bilingual operations team for the cost of one or two full-time hires — without the HR overhead, office costs, or retention risk. If you need more support in Month 3 (e.g., a product launch), we flex up. If you need less in Month 8 (e.g., steady-state operations), we flex down. You only pay for what you need.

Long-Term Sustainability · 长期可持续性

DIY / Internal Team
If your internal Thailand lead leaves, your institutional knowledge walks out the door. You are back to square one — re-hiring, re-training, re-building relationships. Every departure costs 3-6 months of momentum.
Multiple Agencies
Agencies come and go. Contracts expire. Knowledge is siloed. When you switch agencies (which you will — because one of them will inevitably underperform), the new agency has no context. You pay to re-explain your business every 12 months.
ThaiBridge
We are built to be your long-term operating partner. Institutional knowledge lives in our team, not in one person. If a team member transitions, your operations do not skip a beat — because your context is documented in our systems and shared across the team. And if you eventually want to build your own Thailand team, we help you hire and transition — handing over knowledge, relationships, and processes with zero disruption.
How We Work · 我们的工作方式

From zero to operational in Thailand
in five phases.
在泰国从零到运营，五个阶段到位。

A phased approach that de-risks your entry — building foundations before scaling investment. · 分阶段的方法降低进入风险——先打基础，再扩大投资。

1

Foundation (Month 1) · 基础建设（第1个月）

We establish the operational backbone: company registration support (with legal partners), bank account setup, office/co-working space identification, team structure design, and the master project plan with milestones across all workstreams. We conduct the initial market research sprint — store audits, competitor mapping, pricing analysis — to inform all downstream decisions. Output: Master Operations Plan with timeline, budget, and resource allocation. Weekly progress reporting begins.

2

Infrastructure (Month 1-2) · 基础设施（第1-2个月）

We activate the physical and commercial infrastructure: warehouse partner identification and onboarding, product registration and regulatory compliance (FDA, TISI, etc. as applicable), distributor/retail partner sourcing and initial outreach, and creator network strategy and roster building. Every workstream runs in parallel, coordinated by a single project lead. Output: Warehouse operational, regulatory filings submitted, partner shortlists ready for your review.

3

Market Activation (Month 2-3) · 市场激活（第2-3个月）

Your products hit the market: first shipments arrive at the warehouse, e-commerce listings go live (Shopee, Lazada, TikTok Shop), creator campaigns launch with your approved roster, distributor/retail negotiations progress toward agreement, and initial consumer feedback begins flowing in. We manage day-to-day operations across all channels in Thai while you focus on product and strategy. Output: Products live in market. First sales data. First creator content live. Active partner negotiations.

4

Operations & Optimization (Month 3-6) · 运营与优化（第3-6个月）

We shift from launch to optimization: warehouse SLA performance monitoring and improvement, creator campaign iteration based on performance data, distributor/retail partnership stewardship, pricing and promotion optimization, inventory forecasting and replenishment, and customer feedback integration into product and marketing decisions. Monthly Performance Dashboard tracks KPIs across all workstreams. Output: Stable operations. Growing sales. Documented processes. Performance data driving decisions.

5

Scaling & Transition (Month 6-12) · 规模化与交接（第6-12个月）

Strategic phase: expand to additional channels or regions, scale creator roster, deepen distributor relationships, consider local manufacturing or JV opportunities, and — if desired — begin planning your own Thailand team. If you want to build an in-house team, we design the organizational structure, write job descriptions, screen candidates, conduct initial interviews in Thai, and manage a structured knowledge transfer over 2-3 months. If you prefer to continue with us, we operate as your permanent local team — with quarterly strategy reviews and continuous optimization. Output: Your Thailand business runs — with or without us, your choice.

What's the minimum commitment? · 最低合作期限是多久？+
We recommend a minimum 3-month engagement. Month 1 is foundation and infrastructure setup. Month 2 is market activation. By Month 3, you have initial performance data and can assess whether to continue, adjust scope, or transition. Shorter engagements are possible for focused scopes (e.g., "get our warehouse operational and run it for 6 weeks"), but for multi-workstream execution, 3 months is the minimum to deliver meaningful results. Most clients stay with us for 6-12 months. There is no long-term lock-in — we earn your business every month. · 我们建议最低3个月的合作。第1个月是基础和基础设施建设。第2个月是市场激活。到第3个月，您已有初步绩效数据，可以评估是否继续、调整范围或交接。对于聚焦范围（如'让我们的仓库投入运营并运行6周'），更短的项目也可行，但对于多业务线执行，3个月是交付有意义成果的最低时限。大多数客户与我们合作6-12个月。没有长期锁定期——我们每个月都要赢得您的业务。
What costs are NOT included in the retainer? · 哪些费用不包含在服务费中？+
Our monthly retainer covers our team's time, bilingual coordination, project management, and reporting. Costs that are NOT included: warehouse storage and fulfillment fees (paid directly to the warehouse), creator fees (paid directly to creators — we do not mark up), third-party services (legal, accounting, regulatory filing fees), product samples for creators or distributors, travel expenses for site visits outside Bangkok, and advertising spend. We provide a clear budget forecast at the start of each month so you know exactly what additional costs to expect. There are no hidden fees. · 我们的月度服务费覆盖我们团队的时间、双语协调、项目管理和汇报。不包含的费用包括：仓库存储和履约费用（直接支付给仓库）、创作者费用（直接支付给创作者——我们不收取差价）、第三方服务（法律、会计、监管申报费）、给创作者或经销商的产品样品、曼谷以外的差旅费、以及广告投放费用。我们每月初提供清晰的预算预测，让您确切知道会有哪些额外费用。没有任何隐藏费用。
Who is my day-to-day contact? · 我的日常对接人是谁？+
You are assigned a dedicated Operations Lead — a bilingual (Chinese/English and Thai) project manager based in Bangkok. This person is your single point of contact for all workstreams, all questions, and all escalations. They run the weekly progress call, prepare your reports, and coordinate our internal team on your behalf. Behind them, our warehouse, creator, matching, and research specialists execute the actual work. You communicate with one person. Our whole team is behind them. If your Operations Lead is ever unavailable (sick, on leave), a backup is briefed and introduced before the handoff. · 您将被分配一名专属运营主管——一位位于曼谷的双语（中文/英文和泰语）项目经理。此人是您所有业务线、所有问题和所有升级投诉的单一对接人。他们主持每周进度会议，准备您的报告，并代表您协调我们的内部团队。在他们的背后，我们的仓储、创作者、对接和研究专家执行实际工作。您只需与一个人沟通。我们整个团队都在他/她身后。如果您的运营主管临时无法在岗（生病、休假），备选人员会在交接前完成简报和介绍。
Can you help us hire our own Thailand team later? · 你们后续能帮我们组建自己的泰国团队吗？+
Yes — this is a planned transition path for many clients. When you are ready to build your own team, we design the organizational structure, write job descriptions in Thai, screen candidates, conduct initial interviews, and present you with a shortlist. Once you hire, we manage a structured knowledge transfer over 2-3 months: documented processes, relationship introductions, system access, and parallel operations. We do not see your in-house team as competition — we see it as the natural maturation of a successful Thailand entry. Our goal is to make the transition seamless, not to make you dependent on us forever. · 可以——这对很多客户来说是规划的过渡路径。当您准备组建自己的团队时，我们设计组织架构、用泰语编写职位描述、筛选候选人、进行初步面试、并为您提供入围名单。您聘用后，我们在2-3个月内管理结构化的知识转移：文件化的流程、关系引荐、系统访问和并行运营。我们不把您的内部团队视为竞争——我们视其为您泰国市场成功进入的自然成熟标志。我们的目标是让过渡无缝衔接，而不是让您永远依赖我们。
What if we need to scale down or pause? · 如果我们需要缩减规模或暂停怎么办？+
Our retainer model is designed for flexibility. We review scope quarterly (or more frequently if needed). If you need to reduce scope — for example, pausing creator campaigns while maintaining warehouse operations — we adjust the retainer and reallocate team resources accordingly. If you need to pause entirely, we require 30 days' notice and will manage an orderly pause: documenting current state, securing commitments with partners, and preparing a restart plan so you can resume without starting from zero. If your business circumstances change dramatically, we will work with you to find a fair arrangement — we are a partner, not a contract to be enforced. · 我们的服务费模式专为灵活性设计。我们每季度（或根据需要更频繁地）回顾服务范围。如果您需要缩减范围——例如暂时停止创作者营销同时维持仓库运营——我们调整服务费并相应重新分配团队资源。如果您需要完全暂停，我们需要提前30天通知，并将管理有序暂停：记录当前状态、确保合作伙伴承诺、并准备重启计划，以便您能零基础恢复。如果您的业务情况发生重大变化，我们会与您协商找到公平的安排——我们是合作伙伴，不是需要强制执行合同的供应商。
