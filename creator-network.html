Why You Need This

Finding real Thai creators from abroad
is harder than it looks.

Chinese brands waste millions on fake followers and low-engagement campaigns. Here's what actually goes wrong.

01

Can't Tell Real from Fake Influencers

Thai social media is flooded with accounts that have purchased followers, bot-driven engagement, and comment farms. From abroad, you cannot audit audience authenticity — and platforms rarely disclose real engagement data to foreign buyers.

02

Cross-Cultural Briefing Is High-Friction

Writing a creative brief in Chinese and translating it through an agency into Thai loses critical nuance. Tone, cultural references, and humor do not translate. Creators produce content that misses the mark, and you do not know why.

03

Agencies Mark Up Creator Fees 30-50%

Most influencer agencies profit from the spread between what they charge you and what the creator actually receives. A 200,000 THB campaign budget may deliver only 130,000 THB in creator value. You pay the markup without ever knowing it exists.

04

No Long-Term Creator Relationships

Agencies rotate creators between campaigns to maximize their own margins. You never build a roster of trusted creators who understand your brand. Every campaign starts from zero — briefing, negotiating, and hoping the content works.

FAQ

Common questions about our
creator network service.

Straight answers from our campaign team in Bangkok.

How do you verify creator engagement is real?+
We use a three-layer verification process. First, we run every creator through third-party analytics tools (HypeAuditor, SocialBlade, or equivalent) to flag anomalous engagement patterns, sudden follower spikes, and suspicious comment-to-like ratios. Second, we manually review comment quality — are they generic emoji-only comments or do they reflect actual product interest? Third, we track past brand collaboration performance, where available. Creators must pass all three layers to enter our network. We are happy to share verification methodology with clients.
What platforms do you cover?+
Our core platforms are TikTok (including TikTok Shop affiliate content and live commerce), Instagram, and YouTube. We also cover Facebook for certain demographic segments and LINE VOOM for brands targeting the Thai messaging ecosystem. Platform selection is part of our strategy phase — we recommend the right platform mix based on your target audience demographics and campaign objectives. We do not force-fit a TikTok strategy for a B2B brand targeting procurement managers on LinkedIn.
What's the minimum campaign budget?+
We recommend a minimum total budget of 300,000 THB (approximately 60,000 RMB) for a meaningful campaign that includes multiple creators across at least 2 platforms. This typically covers 5-10 creators producing 15-30 pieces of content over 4-8 weeks. Smaller test campaigns are possible but reduce statistical significance and optimization opportunities. Our campaign management fee is quoted separately and scales proportionally — so there is no incentive to inflate your budget.
How long does a typical campaign take from start to finish?+
A typical campaign runs 6-10 weeks: 1-2 weeks for strategy and creator sourcing, 1 week for client selection and contracting, 1 week for briefing, 2-4 weeks for content production and posting, and 1 week for final reporting. Larger campaigns with 20+ creators or multiple content waves may run 10-14 weeks. We can accelerate for product launches with fixed dates — tell us your deadline and we will reverse-engineer the timeline.
Can we reuse the content across our own channels?+
Yes — usage rights are negotiated upfront and clearly documented in every creator contract. We typically secure brand usage rights for organic social reposting, paid advertising (whitelisting / Spark Ads on TikTok), and e-commerce product page usage for a defined period (usually 6-12 months). Extended or perpetual usage rights can be negotiated at additional cost. All rights, terms, and expiration dates are tracked in your Content Asset deliverable so your team always knows what can be used where and until when.
